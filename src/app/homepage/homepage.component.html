This is a fun application with a question form and some statistics.

It does not do a lot - rather, you answer a few elementary questions in the Questionnaire section about your movie preferences and then your responses are being added to the sample of people who have done the same.

In the Statistics section you can see some charts that present the aggregated data based on the number of submitted questionnaires at the time.

Why? ðŸ˜³

A couple of months ago I started a new job and had to learn Angular among other things.

Being used in React for quite some time, I was dreading the transition.

I find building mock applications the most effective way for me to learn new technologies, and this is what I am doing here :)

This app is made with Angular and Amazon Web Services (AWS).

I use DynamoDB to store the questionnaire records and Lambda with API Gateway for creating the endpoints that I use in the frontend to communicate with my database.
